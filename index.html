Hello, we are Anthill Creations

Works

Spread across multiple cities across India and abroad, Anthill Playscapes are transforming the way people perceive play. Beginning our journey of a 1000 miles with a small pilot at IIT Kharagpur in 2015, we have impacted more than 10,000 children in a short course of time and will soon be completing 1000 projects, bringing more smiles to remote, inaccessible corners of the country. Playgrounds made with love, each of them is unique in its own way, designed contextually and integrating teaching elements for learning through play. Using cost effective practices ranging from material exploration of industrial waste to localized sourcing techniques, our playscapes are modular and do-it-yourself, with a start-to-end construction timeline of less than 5 days.

Social

Creating play spaces for children where they gather, interact, learn and develop.

Economic

Adaptive reuse of scrap tires leading to cheaper construction of playgrounds.

Environmental

Awareness of 3R concept.
